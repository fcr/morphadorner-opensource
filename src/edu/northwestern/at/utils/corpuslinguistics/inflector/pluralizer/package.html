Pluralizer.

A pluralizer finds plural forms of nouns and pronouns given a singular form.

All MorphAdorner pluralizers must implement the {@link edu.northwestern.at.utils.corpuslinguistics.inflector.pluralizer.Pluralizer} interface. The {@link edu.northwestern.at.utils.corpuslinguistics.inflector.pluralizer.PluralizerFactory} provides the mechanism for instantiating a default or specified instance of a pluralizer implementation.

MorphAdorner provides two pluralizers.

  • A noop pluralizer, which does nothing.
  • An English language pluralizer.

The English language pluralizer is the default pluralizer.

Pluralizers may be used standalone or instantiated and invoked by an {@link edu.northwestern.at.utils.corpuslinguistics.inflector.Inflector}.
